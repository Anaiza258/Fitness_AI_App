Frequently Asked Questions!

The AI Diet Plan Generator creates a personalized diet plan for you based on the information you provide, including height, weight, age, BMI, and other health factors such as whether you are diabetic. Simply input your details, and the app will generate a customized plan that helps you meet your health and fitness goals.

The Meal Image Analyzer allows you to upload a picture of your meal. Based on the information you’ve already provided in your diet plan, the app analyzes the image to determine if the meal aligns with your dietary needs. It helps ensure that what you're about to eat fits into your personalized plan.

Yes! The chatbot is available for you to ask any questions about your diet plan or the analyzed meals. Whether you need further clarification on your plan or want to discuss your meal options, the chatbot can provide additional information and suggestions.

After generating your diet plan, the app also recommends exercise videos that complement your fitness and health goals. The recommended videos are tailored to your specific needs based on the information you provided, including fitness level, health goals, and diet plan.

Developer FAQs

The AI Diet Plan Generator uses the OpenAI API to analyze user-provided data (such as height, weight, age, BMI, diabetes status, and other health factors). The model generates a personalized diet plan by processing this input data and matching it against nutritional guidelines. The API offers a robust solution for creating diet plans based on the user's unique needs and health goals.

The Meal Image Analyzer utilizes the OpenAI Vision model to process and analyze food images. Users upload images of their meals, which are then passed to the OpenAI Vision model for analysis. The model assesses the meal based on nutritional value, portion size, and how well it fits with the user’s diet plan. This allows the app to evaluate meals for suitability, ensuring that users stay on track with their personalized dietary goals.

The Chatbot is powered by natural language processing (NLP) techniques and integrates directly with the OpenAI API. This allows the chatbot to handle user queries intelligently and provide personalized responses regarding diet plans and meal analysis. The chatbot communicates with the Flask backend to fetch the necessary data for informed responses.
